Earth Lord is a location-based post-apocalyptic survival exploration game. Players need to move in the real world, explore ruins, collect supplies, claim territories, and establish their own sphere of influence in the apocalyptic world.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What should I do if the app cannot locate?

A: Please ensure you have granted the app "Always" or "While Using" location permission in Settings. Path: Settings â†’ Privacy & Security â†’ Location Services â†’ Earth Lord â†’ Always

Q: How to start exploring?

A: Click the "Explore" button at the bottom of the map page. Exploration requires you to move in the real world. The farther you walk, the higher the reward tier you'll receive.

Q: What are the requirements for claiming territory?

A: After clicking "Start Claiming", walk along the boundary of your desired territory to form a closed path. The territory requires at least 3 points and cannot overlap with others' territories.

Q: What happens if I move too fast during exploration?

A: To ensure a genuine walking experience, if speed exceeds 30km/h, the system will issue a warning. If you don't slow down within 10 seconds, the exploration will fail.

Q: How to switch language?

A: Go to the "Settings" page, click on "Language" option, and select Simplified Chinese or English.

Q: What if I can't find nearby POIs?

A: POIs (Points of Interest) are displayed based on real locations around you. If there are no facilities like hospitals or supermarkets nearby, POIs may not appear. Please move to an area with more facilities.
