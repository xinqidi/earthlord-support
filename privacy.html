Welcome to Earth Lord

We take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how Earth Lord ("we," "the app," or "the service") collects, uses, stores, and protects your personal information.

Important: By using this app, you agree to this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with any part of this policy, please do not use the app.

1. Information Collection

1.1 Information We Collect

To provide gaming services, we may collect the following information:

  • Location Information: Your real-time GPS coordinates for exploration, territory claiming, and displaying nearby points of interest (POI)
  • Account Information: Email address and username provided through Google Sign-In
  • Game Data: Your territory coordinates, exploration records, collected items, inventory contents
  • Device Information: Device model, OS version, app version
  • Usage Data: App usage patterns, feature usage frequency, error logs

1.2 Use of Location Information

We collect your location information for the following purposes:

  • Display your current location on the map
  • Calculate exploration walking distance and reward tiers
  • Record boundary coordinates of your claimed territories
  • Search and display nearby points of interest (POI)
  • Detect proximity to POIs to trigger scavenging events
  • Display other players' territory locations (anonymously)

1.3 Information We Don't Collect

We do not collect the following information:

  • Your contacts, SMS, or call logs
  • Your photos or media files
  • Your microphone or camera data (unless you actively use related features)
  • Your payment information (if in-app purchases are involved, handled by Apple)

2. Information Usage

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Provide Core Game Features: Exploration, territory claiming, item collection, territory management
  • Improve User Experience: Optimize app performance, fix bugs, develop new features
  • Security and Anti-Cheating: Detect abnormal behavior, prevent GPS spoofing, protect game fairness
  • Data Analysis: Understand user behavior, optimize game balance
  • Customer Support: Respond to your questions and feedback

3. Information Storage and Security

3.1 Data Storage

Your data is stored on Supabase cloud servers, which comply with the following security standards:

  • Data transmission using SSL/TLS encryption
  • Database using Row Level Security (RLS) policies
  • Regular backups and disaster recovery mechanisms
  • Compliance with international privacy standards like GDPR and CCPA

3.2 Data Retention

We will retain your data until:

  • You delete your account
  • You haven't used the app for 90 days (we may delete inactive accounts)
  • Required by law to delete

3.3 Security Measures

We take the following measures to protect your information:

  • Encrypted storage of passwords and sensitive data
  • Access control and authentication
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability scans
  • Employee privacy training and confidentiality agreements

4. Information Sharing

We will not sell your personal information.

We only share your information in the following circumstances:

  • In-Game Display: Your territory coordinates and username are visible to other players on the map (this is a core game feature)
  • Service Providers: Third-party services we use (such as Supabase, Google Sign-In) need access to specific data to provide services
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law or to protect our rights, property, or safety
  • Business Transactions: If a merger, acquisition, or asset sale occurs, your information may be transferred (we will notify you in advance)

5. Third-Party Services

This app uses the following third-party services:

  • Supabase: Database and backend services | Privacy Policy
  • Google Sign-In: Authentication service | Privacy Policy
  • Apple MapKit: Map display service | Privacy Policy

These third-party services have their own privacy policies. We recommend reading their policies to understand how they handle your data.

6. Children's Privacy

This app is not intended for children under 13 years old. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and discover that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will immediately delete such information.

7. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Right of Access: You can request access to personal information we hold about you
  • Right to Rectification: You can request correction of inaccurate information
  • Right to Deletion: You can request deletion of your account and all related data
  • Right to Data Portability: You can request to export your data in a structured, commonly used format
  • Right to Object: You can object to certain data processing activities
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: You can withdraw consent to data processing at any time

To exercise these rights, please contact us at support@earthlord.app.

8. Location Permissions

This app requires "Always" or "While Using" location permission to function properly. You can change permission settings in device settings at any time. Please note:

  • Denying location permission will disable exploration and territory claiming features
  • We only actively collect location information when you use the app
  • We do not continuously track your location in the background (unless you are exploring)
  • "Always" permission is used to support geofencing features (POI proximity notifications)

9. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

This app does not use cookies or web tracking technologies. We only store necessary app settings (such as language preferences) locally on the device.

10. International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and stored on servers outside your country/region. By using this app, you consent to such transfers. We ensure that your data is adequately protected during transfer.

11. Privacy Policy Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If there are significant changes, we will notify you within the app. Continued use of the app indicates your acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.

You can always review this page for the latest Privacy Policy. The update date is displayed at the top of the page.

12. Contact Us

If you have any questions, comments, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us:
